Phoenix

Role: Duelist

Phoenix is a fiery duelist with unparalleled control over flames. He thrives on aggressive plays and excels at disrupting enemies with his powerful abilities.

Phoenix's kit is perfect for players who enjoy high-risk, high-reward gameplay. With abilities like curveball flashes, healing flames, and his ultimate resurrection, he is a force to be reckoned with in any match.

Whether you're pushing onto a site or defending key positions, Phoenix provides versatility and power that can turn the tide of any round.

Performance Stats

  • Win Rate: 75%
  • Pick Rate: 45%
  • Average K/D Ratio: 1.25

Key Abilities

Ability Description
Curveball Blinds enemies with a fiery flashbang that curves left or right.
Hot Hands Throws a fireball that heals Phoenix or damages enemies.
Blaze Creates a wall of fire that blocks vision and damages enemies.
Run It Back Phoenix revives if killed during this ability's duration.

About Us

Palorant is a 5v5 tactical FPS game designed for PC gamers. Each match challenges players to utilize unique skills, strategies, and teamwork to dominate the battlefield. With a roster of diverse agents and detailed maps, Palorant combines action-packed gameplay with intense strategic depth, making it a favorite among competitive gamers worldwide.
